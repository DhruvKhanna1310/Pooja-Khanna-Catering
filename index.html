About

A little about the kitchen, the cooking style, and what you can expect with every order.

Hi, I’m Pooja.

Cooking has always been a big part of my life. What started as preparing meals for my family and friends has now grown into something I’m excited to share with a wider community. From my home kitchen, I’ve begun taking larger food orders and turning my passion into a small, heartfelt venture.

I specialize in North Indian meals, festive sweets, and homemade snacks, all cooked the way I would for my own family — fresh, comforting, and made with care. The warm response I’ve received so far has encouraged me to dream bigger, with a long-term vision of growing this into a cloud kitchen, so more people in and around OSC can enjoy healthy, home-style food.

To me, food is about connection. It brings people together, creates memories, and adds joy to everyday moments and celebrations alike. Through this journey, I hope to not just serve good food, but also create experiences that feel warm, familiar, and special every time.
